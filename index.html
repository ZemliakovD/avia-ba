Our global client base includes private and commercial airlines, airports, lessors, handling agencies and other aviation companies. As an independent advisory firm with no joint ventures or other business affiliations, we pride ourselves on offering our clients independent and objective counsel.

At AVIA BA, our purpose is to deliver the professional, data-driven guidance, innovative tailor-made solution and quantitative analytics. We are here to help our clients when they require to identify problems, analyze options, support process management, prioritize resources and manage risk by finding the right solution and bringing out the best in every aviation challenge you might have in mind.
